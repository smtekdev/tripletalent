About Triple Talent

For over 16 years, Triple Talent Musical Theater has been nurturing and developing the talents of young actors in the West Valley through professional children's theater productions, Musical Theater and Hip-Hop Summer Camps, and Arts-in-Education programs featured in numerous local schools.

We believe every child has a unique talent waiting to be discovered and refined. Our comprehensive programs combine voice training, acting techniques, dance, and performance skills to create well-rounded young performers ready to take on any stage.

Neisha Folkes

Founder & Artistic Director

With decades of experience in musical theater and education, Neisha brings unparalleled expertise and passion to every student. Her vision has transformed hundreds of young performers into confident, skilled artists ready to shine on any stage.
